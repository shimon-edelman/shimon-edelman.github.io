Cycling around Ithaca, NY

Mostly on the roads, but there are some "low-maintenance" gravel-surface roads as well.

All mileage figures are for round trips starting and terminating at the North-East Elementary School (Winthrop Rd, Ithaca, NY 14850). Ithaca maps
