Psych 6140 essay

Write between 1000 and 3000 words, aiming to pull together one or more of the conceptual threads — neural computation, hierarchical abstraction, statistical inference, etc. — that run through the material covered by the book, the lectures, and the supplementary readings.

You may structure your write-up as an article in the science section of the New York Times, a feature in the New Yorker, a Scientific American article, a book review (which does not have to be positive, but must be well-argued and well-supported by references), an introduction to a book (which may be real or imaginary), an epic poem in true or alliterative verse (no free verse, please), or in any other manner that you may choose.

It would probably be a good idea for you to talk to me (during office hours) before starting to write.
